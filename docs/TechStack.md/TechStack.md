# Artificial Intelligence

## OpenAI API

Purpose:

Natural language understanding and generation.

---

## LangGraph

Purpose:

Multi-agent orchestration.

Reason:

Allows AI employees to collaborate and complete complex workflows.

---

## MCP (Model Context Protocol)

Purpose:

Secure communication between AI models and external tools.

Reason:

Provides a standardized way for AI agents to access business systems, APIs, files, and databases while maintaining clear boundaries and permissions.

---

## RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

Purpose:

Enterprise knowledge retrieval.

Reason:

Allows AI employees to answer questions using company-specific documents and data rather than relying only on general model knowledge.

---

## Vector Database

Purpose:

Semantic search and memory.

Reason:

Stores embeddings for documents, conversations, and business knowledge to improve AI responses.

---

## Hugging Face

Purpose:

Open-source AI models.

Reason:

Supports specialized models for language, vision, and other AI tasks.

---

## PyTorch

Purpose:

Machine learning and deep learning.

Reason:

Flexible framework for building and training advanced AI models.
